Valuation Why MDCopia

The Playing Field Should Be Level.

MDCopia is the only physician practice marketplace where buyers and sellers start from the same valuation.

For Practice Owners

Your Practice. Your Price. Your Decision.

The problem: Most physicians who sell their practice negotiate blind. They don't know what their practice is worth, and the buyer (often a PE-backed platform with a dedicated M&A team) does. The information asymmetry is structural, and it costs physicians hundreds of thousands of dollars.

The MDCopia solution: We produce an independent valuation range using public utilization data, local comparable transactions, and specialty-specific multipliers. This range is shared with both the seller and the buyer before negotiations begin. The buyer contractually agrees to start negotiations within this range — not to close there, but to start there.

The data promise: We do not share your information with anyone without your explicit, verified consent. You see your valuation before you decide whether to enter the marketplace. You can withdraw at any time.

For Buyers

Better Leads. Realistic Expectations. Faster Closes.

The problem for buyers: Cold outreach to physician practices has a low hit rate. When it does work, the physician often has unrealistic price expectations (too high or too low), which wastes months of due diligence.

The MDCopia solution: Every lead comes pre-qualified with a data-driven valuation range. The physician has already seen and accepted this range as the starting point for negotiations. This eliminates the most common reason deals fall apart: misaligned expectations on day one.

What you receive: Seller contact information, the MDCopia valuation memo (including methodology and data sources), and all available market research for the practice's geography and specialty.

Our Methodology

Data-Driven. Locally Calibrated.

MDCopia's valuation methodology draws on multiple public and proprietary data sources, including CMS Medicare utilization and payment data, the NPPES National Provider Identifier registry, state medical board licensing records, local comparable transaction databases, Bureau of Labor Statistics physician compensation benchmarks, and Census Bureau demographic data for the practice's service area. These inputs are processed through proprietary specialty-specific multipliers that are continuously refined using 12-month post-transaction outcome data from completed deals on the platform.

Your Data. Your Control.

We collect only what we need to produce your valuation. We do not track you, cookie you, or fingerprint your device. Your practice information is never shared with any buyer until you have (1) seen your valuation, (2) verified your email, and (3) given explicit written consent. You can request deletion of your data at any time.
