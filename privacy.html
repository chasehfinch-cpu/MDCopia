Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-01

1. What we collect

The specific data fields from the valuation form: practice name, specialty, city, state, NPI (optional), annual revenue, annual visits, number of sites, real estate status, timeline to sell, and (when you choose to proceed) your contact email. Nothing else. No cookies, no tracking, no device fingerprinting. Your valuation range is computed entirely in your browser before any data is sent to MDCopia.

2. How we use it

Seller data is used exclusively to produce a valuation range and, with your consent, to connect you with a qualified buyer. Seller contact information is shared with a buyer ONLY after: (a) you have received and reviewed your valuation range, (b) you have verified your email address, (c) you have provided explicit written consent to be connected with buyers, and (d) the buyer has been vetted by MDCopia and has agreed to the negotiation range obligation.

3. What we build and distribute

Valuation memos: We produce a valuation range document based on public data sources (CMS Medicare utilization data, NPI registry, state licensing databases, county property records, and comparable transaction databases). This memo is shared with the buyer as part of the lead package, but ONLY after seller consent.

Market research: Aggregated, anonymized market data about practice valuations in a given geography and specialty. This may be shared with buyers to support their acquisition strategy. This research never contains individually identifiable seller information.

4. The negotiation range agreement

Buyers who purchase leads agree to initiate negotiations within the valuation range provided by MDCopia. This obligation applies to the opening of negotiations, not the final transaction price. Buyers who consistently open below the provided range may be removed from the platform. MDCopia does not guarantee that a transaction will close, does not represent either party in negotiations, and is not a licensed business broker.

5. Data retention

Seller data is retained for the duration of your engagement with the platform plus 12 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@mdcopia.com. Buyer data is retained for the duration of their engagement.

6. Data security

Data is stored in Google Workspace (Google Sheets) with access restricted to MDCopia principals. No data is stored in third-party databases, CRMs, or analytics platforms. All form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS. Email verification codes expire after 30 minutes and are not reusable.

7. What we will never do

  • Sell, rent, or trade seller or buyer data to any third party.
  • Share seller data with a buyer who has not been vetted and who has not agreed to the negotiation range obligation.
  • Use seller data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than producing a valuation and facilitating a buyer connection at your request.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries, email hello@mdcopia.com.
